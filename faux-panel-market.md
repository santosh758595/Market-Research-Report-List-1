<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Faux panel market types refer to different types of materials used in the manufacturing of fake panels that resemble real wood. Particleboard (PB) is made from wood particles bonded with resin, while Density Fiberboard is made from finely ground wood fibers and resin. Hardboard is a type of composite panel made from wood fibers pressed together. Oriented Strand Board (OSB) is made from strands of wood compressed together with resin. Plywood is made from thin layers of wood veneers glued together. Finally, "Others" represents any other materials that are used in faux panel production.</p></p>
