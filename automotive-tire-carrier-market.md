<p><ul><li>Bumper Mounted Tire Carriers</li><li>Tailgate Reinforcement Style Tire Carriers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive tire carriers are available in two primary types: bumper mounted tire carriers and tailgate reinforcement style tire carriers. Bumper mounted tire carriers are designed to securely hold the spare tire at the back of the vehicle, attached to the rear bumper. On the other hand, tailgate reinforcement style tire carriers provide additional support to the tailgate and allow for the spare tire to be mounted directly on the tailgate itself. Both types ensure the safe and convenient transportation of spare tires while maximizing vehicle space and accessibility.</p></p>
