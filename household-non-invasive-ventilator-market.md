<p><p>The Household Non-invasive Ventilator Market consists of three main types: CPAP, BiPAP, and APAP. CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) is a device that delivers a constant pressure for individuals with sleep apnea to ensure their airway remains open during sleep. BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) offers different air pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation, making it suitable for patients with breathing difficulties. APAP (Auto-Adjusting Positive Airway Pressure) automatically adjusts the pressure based on the patient's breathing patterns, providing personalized therapy. These devices provide effective respiratory support without the need for invasive procedures.</p></p>
