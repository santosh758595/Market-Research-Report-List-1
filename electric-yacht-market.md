<p><p>The electric yacht market can be divided into two main types: pure electric yachts and hybrid power yachts. Pure electric yachts run solely on electric power and do not rely on fossil fuels, offering zero emissions and a more sustainable alternative. Hybrid power yachts, on the other hand, combine electric power with traditional fuel-powered engines. This allows for increased range and flexibility, as the yacht can switch between electric and fuel power as needed while still reducing overall emissions compared to traditional yachts.</p></p>
