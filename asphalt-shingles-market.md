There are three types of asphalt shingles available in the market: high-performance laminated, laminated, and three-tab shingles. High-performance laminated shingles are known for their enhanced durability, weather resistance, and longer lifespan. Laminated shingles offer a similar level of durability but at a more affordable price point. Three-tab shingles are the most basic option, providing reliable protection but with less aesthetic appeal.
