<p><p>PET strapping refers to the use of polyethylene terephthalate (PET) material for securing goods during transportation or storage. There are two main types of PET strapping: embossed and smooth. Embossed PET strapping has raised patterns or textures on its surface, providing enhanced grip and preventing slippage when securing heavy or irregularly shaped loads. On the other hand, smooth PET strapping is characterized by a smooth surface, offering high tensile strength and excellent flexibility, making it suitable for various types of loads. Both types are durable, cost-effective, and widely used in industries such as logistics, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
