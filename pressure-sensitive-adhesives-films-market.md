<p><p>Pressure sensitive adhesive films are used for their ability to adhere to surfaces when light pressure is applied. These films can be categorized into different types based on their composition. Acrylic films offer excellent adhesion to a wide range of surfaces and have good UV resistance. Rubber films provide high tack and flexibility, making them suitable for uneven surfaces. Silicone films have high temperature resistance and are non-toxic. Ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA) films offer low-temperature flexibility and good adhesion. Other types of pressure sensitive adhesive films include polyurethane, polyester, and polyamide films, each with their own unique properties.</p></p>
