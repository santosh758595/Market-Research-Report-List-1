<p><p>X-ray protective gloves market consists of two main types: lead gloves and lead-free gloves. Lead gloves are made with a combination of lead and rubber materials, providing effective radiation protection. They are widely used in areas like radiology and nuclear medicine. They are a newer option in the market and are gaining popularity due to environmental and health concerns associated with the use of lead.</p></p>
