<p><p>A movable aerobridge, also known as an aircraft boarding bridge or jet bridge, is a movable passageway that connects the terminal building to an aircraft, allowing passengers to board or disembark without the need to go outside. The increasing demand for air travel, coupled with the expansion of airports worldwide, is driving the market growth. Movable aerobridges provide several benefits, including enhanced passenger experience, improved safety, and increased operational efficiency. These factors are contributing to the adoption of movable aerobridges by airports globally.</p><p>Moreover, the rising focus on passenger convenience and the need to reduce turnaround time for aircraft are fueling the market growth. Airports are increasingly investing in advanced and technologically advanced movable aerobridges to provide a seamless boarding experience for passengers.
