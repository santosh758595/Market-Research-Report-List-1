<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Parachute Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Round</li><li>Cruciform</li><li>Annular and pull down apex</li><li>Rogallo wing</li><li>Ribbon and Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are different types of parachutes available in the market. Round parachutes are traditional and offer simplicity and durability. Cruciform parachutes have a cross-shaped design that provides stability during descent. Annular parachutes have a circular shape and provide high maneuverability. Pull-down apex parachutes combine the stability of round parachutes with the maneuverability of cruciform ones. Rogallo wing parachutes offer high maneuverability and are used mainly for paragliding. Ribbon and ring parachutes are designed for high-speed and high-altitude applications, offering stability and control.</p></p>
