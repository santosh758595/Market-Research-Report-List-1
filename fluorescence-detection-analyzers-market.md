<p><p>Fluorescence detection analyzers are devices used for identifying and measuring the presence of fluorescent substances in various samples. They are available in two types: fully automatic and semiautomatic. Fully automatic analyzers are highly automated systems that perform all functions, from sample preparation to analysis, with minimal human intervention. They offer high throughput and precision, making them suitable for large-scale laboratories. On the other hand, semiautomatic analyzers require some level of manual intervention during the analytical process. They are more suitable for smaller-scale laboratories or applications that do not require high throughput.</p></p>
