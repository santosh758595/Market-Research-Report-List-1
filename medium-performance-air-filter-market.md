<p><p>The medium performance air filter market includes various types of filters. Panel/pleated air filters consist of a flat or pleated filter media that is enclosed in a cardboard frame. Pocket/bag air filters have multiple pockets or bags made of filter material, which are stitched together to form the filter. Box air filters have a box-like structure with filter media inside. Cartridges air filters are cylindrical in shape and usually made of pleated filter media. These filters are designed to remove medium-sized particles from the air and are commonly used in HVAC systems.</p></p>
