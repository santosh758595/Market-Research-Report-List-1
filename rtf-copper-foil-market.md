<p><p>RTF Copper Foil market is categorized into three types based on thickness: below 20 μm, 20-50 μm, and above 50 μm. Copper foil with thickness below 20 μm is widely used in flexible printed circuit boards (PCBs), as it provides high conductivity and promotes miniaturization. Meanwhile, the 20-50 μm thickness range is suitable for various applications, including lithium-ion batteries and electromagnetic shielding. Copper foil with thickness above 50 μm is primarily used in power electronics and automotive industries, where high current carrying capacity is required.</p></p>
