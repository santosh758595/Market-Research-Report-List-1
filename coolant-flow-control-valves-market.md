<p><strong>The Coolant Flow Control Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Way</li><li>3 Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coolant flow control valves are used in various industries to regulate the flow of coolant in a system. Two-way valves have two ports, one for the inlet and one for the outlet, allowing or stopping the coolant flow. They are commonly used to control the flow direction or isolate a section of the system. On the other hand, three-way valves have three ports, enabling the diversion of the coolant flow to alternate paths. This type is often employed for flow direction control or mixing different fluids within the system.</p></p>
