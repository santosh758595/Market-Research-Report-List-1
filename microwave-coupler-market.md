<p><strong>The Microwave Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coupled Transmission Line</li><li>Independent Transmission Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microwave coupler market consists of two types: coupled transmission line market and independent transmission line market. In the coupled transmission line market, couplers are designed to be interconnected with the main transmission line, allowing power to be divided or combined. These couplers are used in applications such as power dividers and directional couplers. On the other hand, the independent transmission line market includes couplers that operate independently of the main transmission line, making them suitable for applications like isolators and circulators.</p></p>
