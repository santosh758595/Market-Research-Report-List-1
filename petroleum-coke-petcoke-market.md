<p><p>Petroleum Coke, or Petcoke, is a solid carbon-rich byproduct derived from oil refining. It is categorized into four market types: Needle Coke, Shot Coke, Sponge Coke, and Honeycomb Coke. Needle Coke has a needle-like structure and is used in the production of graphite electrodes. Shot Coke is spherical and is utilized in the manufacturing of titanium dioxide. Sponge Coke has a sponge-like structure and is suitable for fuel applications. Honeycomb Coke has a honeycomb structure and has applications in the production of fuel cells or catalysts. These different coke types serve various industrial purposes.</p></p>
