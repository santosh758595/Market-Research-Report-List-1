<p><p>Commercial baking ovens come in different types, including convection ovens and deck ovens. Convection ovens use fans to circulate hot air, leading to faster and more even cooking. They are often used for baking pastries, bread, and other delicate goods. On the other hand, deck ovens feature multiple shelves or "decks" where products can be placed directly on stone or metal surfaces. This type of oven is commonly used for baking pizzas, bread, and other items requiring a crisp and evenly baked crust.</p></p>
