<p><p>A nonrebreathing oxygen mask is a medical device used for patients who require high concentrations of oxygen. It is designed to deliver oxygen to the patient without re-inhaling exhaled gases, ensuring that the patient receives only fresh oxygen. This type of mask consists of a mask, reservoir bag, and one-way valves. The reservoir bag stores oxygen and helps to maintain a constant supply to the patient.</p><p>The nonrebreathing oxygen mask market is growing rapidly due to the increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases and the rising number of surgeries worldwide.
