<p><p>The automotive exhaust gas sensors market consists of various types of sensors used in vehicles to monitor and measure the exhaust gas emissions. O2-Lambda sensors, also known as oxygen sensors, measure the oxygen level in the exhaust gas to ensure optimal combustion. NOX sensors detect and measure nitrogen oxide emissions. Particulate matter sensors monitor and control the level of particulate matter in the exhaust gas. Differential pressure sensors measure the pressure difference across a filter or catalyst. Temperature sensors monitor the temperature of the exhaust gas. There are also other types of sensors used in the market to monitor different exhaust gas parameters.</p></p>
