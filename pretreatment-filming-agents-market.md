<p><ul><li>Inorganic Anti Corrosion Agents</li><li>Organic Anti Corrosion Agents</li><li>Polymer Anti Corrosion Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pretreatment filming agents are used to protect metal surfaces from corrosion. There are three types of these agents - inorganic anti-corrosion agents, organic anti-corrosion agents, and polymer anti-corrosion agents. Inorganic agents contain compounds like chromates or phosphates, which form a protective film on the metal surface. Organic agents, on the other hand, use organic compounds to form a protective layer on the metal. Polymer agents involve the application of polymers that firmly adhere to the metal surface, providing excellent corrosion resistance. All these types of agents help extend the lifespan of metal objects.</p></p>
