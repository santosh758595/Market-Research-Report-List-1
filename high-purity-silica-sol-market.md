<p><p>High Purity Silica Sol is available in two different types: Hydrophilic Solvent and Hydrophobic Solvent. The Hydrophilic Solvent type of High Purity Silica Sol has a strong affinity for water molecules and can dissolve easily in water-based systems. It is widely used in industries such as coatings, electronic chemicals, and papermaking. On the other hand, the Hydrophobic Solvent type repels water and is commonly used in oil-based systems. It finds applications in areas like catalysts, lubricants, and antifoaming agents. Both types possess high purity and offer diverse benefits based on their compatibility with water or oil.</p></p>
