<p><p>Automobiles heated windshields market includes two types: heated wire windshields and heated coated windshields. Heated wire windshields consist of thin wire embedded in the glass that heats up to melt ice or snow. They are cost-effective and widely used. On the other hand, heated coated windshields have a thin electrically conductive layer on the glass, which quickly heats up using electrical energy. They are efficient and provide better visibility. Both types of heated windshields serve the purpose of ensuring a clear and safe view while driving in cold weather conditions.</p></p>
