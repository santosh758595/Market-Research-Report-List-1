<p><p>The conference microphone system market consists of two main types: wired and wireless. In the wired market, microphones are connected to the audio system using physical cables, providing a stable and reliable connection. On the other hand, the wireless market utilizes radio frequency technology to transmit audio signals from microphones to a receiver, allowing for greater mobility and flexibility. Each type has its advantages and disadvantages, with wired systems typically offering better audio quality and reliability, while wireless systems offer convenience and ease of use.</p></p>
