<p><p>Electrically Tunable-Focusing Lenses (ETFL) are a type of lenses that can change their focus by applying electrical signals. In the market, there are two main types of ETFL lenses: Flat Convex and Flat Concave. Flat Convex lenses have a curved surface facing away from the observer, allowing them to focus light rays to a single point. On the other hand, Flat Concave lenses have a curved surface facing towards the observer, causing light rays to diverge. Both lens types provide adjustable focus capabilities through electrical control.</p></p>
