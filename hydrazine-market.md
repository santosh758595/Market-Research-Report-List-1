<p><p>The hydrazine market consists of three main types: Ultra-Pure Hydrazine, Anhydrous Hydrazine, and Hydrazine Hydrate. Ultra-Pure Hydrazine refers to highly refined hydrazine with a purity level of over 99.5%, commonly used in industries such as electronics and pharmaceuticals. Anhydrous Hydrazine is a water-free form of hydrazine, often utilized as a rocket propellant and in fuel cells. Hydrazine Hydrate is a water-based solution of hydrazine and typically contains 80% hydrazine, widely employed in various applications such as water treatment, agricultural chemicals, and polymerization catalysts.</p></p>
