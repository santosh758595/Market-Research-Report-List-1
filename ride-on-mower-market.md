<p><p>The ride-on mower market comprises two main types: gasoline ride-on mowers and electric ride-on mowers. Gasoline ride-on mowers are powered by internal combustion engines, which require gasoline as fuel. These mowers offer more power and are suitable for larger areas. On the other hand, electric ride-on mowers are powered by rechargeable batteries, providing a quieter and eco-friendly option. They are typically recommended for smaller areas due to their lower power output. Both types cater to different customer preferences, with gasoline mowers offering greater strength and electric mowers prioritizing environmentally friendly features.</p></p>
