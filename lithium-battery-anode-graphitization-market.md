<p><p>The Lithium Battery Anode Graphitization market consists of two main types: the Direct Method and the Indirect Method. In the Direct Method, the anode material is directly graphitized by heating it at high temperatures, allowing for the efficient production of graphitized anode materials. On the other hand, the Indirect Method involves the carbonization of the anode material before graphitization, resulting in higher purity and better graphitization. Both methods play a crucial role in the production of graphitized anode materials for lithium batteries.</p></p>
