<p><p>The Spinal Electrical Stimulation Devices market includes two types: Rechargeable SCS and Non-Rechargeable SCS. They often have a higher upfront cost but can be cost-effective in the long run. Non-Rechargeable SCS devices, on the other hand, are disposable and provide temporary pain relief for a set period before needing replacement.
