<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The market for multifunctional high frequency electrotomes can be divided into two types: monopolar circuit and bipolar circuit. In the monopolar circuit market, electrotomes are used with a single active electrode and a grounding pad, allowing for cutting, coagulation, and desiccation of tissues. In the bipolar circuit market, electrotomes are used with two electrodes that work in close proximity to minimize tissue damage during surgery. Both types of electrotomes provide surgeons with versatile tools for various surgical procedures.</p></p>
