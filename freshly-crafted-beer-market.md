<p><p>The freshly crafted beer market can be broadly categorized into two main types: Lager and Ale. Lager refers to a type of beer that is fermented and conditioned at lower temperatures, resulting in a clean and crisp taste. It is known for its light color and flavor, making it a popular choice among beer drinkers. On the other hand, Ale is brewed using a warm fermentation process, which gives it a fuller body and higher alcohol content. Ale beers often have a wide range of flavors and aromas, making them more diverse in taste compared to lagers.</p></p>
