<p><p>The Aluminum Trifluoride (AlF3) market is categorized into two types: Dry AlF3 and Wet AlF3. Dry AlF3 is a powdered form of AlF3, which is produced by heating aluminum hydroxide with hydrogen fluoride gas. It is commonly used in the production of aluminum metal and as a catalyst in various chemical reactions. Wet AlF3 refers to the AlF3 dissolved in water, forming a liquid solution. This type of AlF3 is mainly utilized as a flux in the production of aluminum, helping to remove impurities and improve the metal's quality.</p></p>
