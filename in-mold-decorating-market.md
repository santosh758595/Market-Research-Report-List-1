<p><p>In-mold decorating is a technique used to apply graphics or decorations to plastic products during the manufacturing process. It involves placing a pre-printed label or film with the desired design into the mold before injecting or blow molding the plastic material. The rising demand for aesthetically appealing and customized products, particularly in the packaging industry, is driving the market growth. In-mold decorating offers numerous benefits such as high-quality printing, resistance to scratches and abrasions, and ability to apply complex designs, making it a preferred choice for various applications.</p><p>Key market trends in the in-mold decorating industry include advancements in printing technologies, increasing adoption of sustainable materials, and growing demand for consumer electronics and automotive products.
