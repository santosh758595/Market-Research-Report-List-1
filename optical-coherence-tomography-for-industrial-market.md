<p><p>Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) is an imaging technology used for non-destructive testing in various industrial applications. There are two main types: 2D-OCT and 3D-OCT. The 2D-OCT, also known as a B-Scan, provides cross-sectional images of an object or material, allowing for precise analysis of its internal structures. On the other hand, the 3D-OCT generates a three-dimensional representation, enabling detailed volumetric inspection and measurements. Both market types offer reliable and accurate imaging capabilities, making them valuable assets in industrial quality control, defect detection, and characterization processes.</p></p>
