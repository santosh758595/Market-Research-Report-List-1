C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering) is integrated into the steering column, P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering) is integrated into the steering gear mechanism, and R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering) is integrated into the steering rack. These systems provide varying levels of assistance to the driver and improve steering control in different ways, catering to the diverse needs of the automotive market.</p></p>
