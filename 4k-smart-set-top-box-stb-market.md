<p><p>The 4K Smart Set Top Box (STB) market is categorized into different types based on the technology used for content delivery. IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) refers to the transmission of television programming through internet protocol networks. Satellite STB receives television signals from satellites. DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television) STB receives digital signals from terrestrial broadcast networks. OTT (Over-The-Top) STB allows streaming content directly from the internet. Hybrid STB combines multiple technologies, like IPTV and DTT, to provide a wider range of services and content options to users.</p></p>
