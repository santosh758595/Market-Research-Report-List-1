<p><p>Mobile C-arm Systems are medical imaging devices used in surgeries and interventional procedures to provide real-time X-ray imaging. They come in different types, such as Little C-arms and Peripheral Interventional C-arm systems. Little C-arms are smaller and more portable, designed for orthopedic surgeries and procedures in smaller operating rooms. Peripheral Interventional C-arm systems, on the other hand, are larger and specialized for vascular and other interventional procedures. These systems greatly enhance the accuracy and efficiency of surgical interventions by allowing surgeons to visualize anatomical structures in real-time.</p></p>
