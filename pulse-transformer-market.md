<p><p>The pulse transformer market can be categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I pulse transformers are primarily used in applications where a low voltage input pulse is transformed into a high voltage output pulse. They are commonly found in power supplies, ignition systems, and telecommunication equipment. On the other hand, Type II pulse transformers are used in applications where a high voltage input pulse is converted into a low voltage output pulse. They are widely used in medical equipment, laser systems, and industrial automation devices.</p></p>
