<p><p>The traditional lead-acid storage battery is the most common type found in automotive applications. It consists of lead plates immersed in an acidic electrolyte solution, and requires regular maintenance such as checking fluid levels and recharging. On the other hand, the maintenance-free storage battery, also known as sealed or maintenance-free, is designed to be maintenance-free throughout its lifespan. It does not require fluid level checks or recharging and is sealed to prevent leakage, making it a convenient and hassle-free option for automotive use.</p></p>
