<p><p>Aerospace cold forgings market comprises three types: custom forging, captive forging, and catalog forging. Custom forging involves the production of forgings based on specific requirements and designs of aerospace companies. Captive forging refers to forgings produced by aerospace companies themselves for their own use. Catalog forging involves the production of forgings based on standardized designs and specifications, which are available for purchase in a catalog by aerospace companies. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of the aerospace industry in terms of forging production and procurement.</p></p>
