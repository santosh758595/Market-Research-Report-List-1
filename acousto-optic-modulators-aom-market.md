<p><p>Acousto-optic modulators (AOM) are devices that enable control and modulation of laser beams by using the interaction of sound waves and light. There are two main types of AOM market: fiber-coupled and free-space AOMs. Fiber-coupled AOMs are designed to couple the laser light into a single-mode fiber, allowing for efficient and compact setups in fiber-optic systems. On the other hand, free-space AOMs operate by modulating the laser beam directly without the need for fiber coupling, making them suitable for applications that require flexibility and larger beam apertures.</p></p>
