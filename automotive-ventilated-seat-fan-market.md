<p><p>The automotive ventilated seat fan market consists of two main types: axial fans and radial fans. Axial fans are designed with blades that rotate around an axis, creating a flow of air in a straight line from the input to the output. They are commonly used in vehicle ventilation systems. On the other hand, radial fans have blades that rotate radially outward from the center, pushing air in a direction perpendicular to the axis of rotation. These fans are also commonly used in automotive applications for cooling purposes.</p></p>
