<p><ul><li>Semi Automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial smart laminator market comprises two types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. A semi-automatic laminator requires some manual intervention such as loading the laminating sheet and removing the finished product, while the rest of the process is automated. On the other hand, a fully automatic laminator eliminates the need for manual involvement by automatically feeding the laminating sheet and completing the laminating process. Both types offer convenience and efficiency in commercial laminating operations, with the fully automatic variant providing a higher level of automation and reducing reliance on human intervention.</p></p>
