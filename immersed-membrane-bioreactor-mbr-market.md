<p><p>An Immersed Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) is a wastewater treatment system that combines the activated sludge process with membrane filtration technology. In an MBR system, the membrane module is submerged directly into the mixed liquor, allowing the separation of treated water and sludge. This membrane barrier traps suspended solids, bacteria, and pathogens, producing high-quality treated water that can be reused for various purposes.</p><p>The Immersed Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) Market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. <p><p>The Immersed Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) market includes various types such as Hollow Fiber Membrane, Flat Sheet Membrane, and Multi-Tubular Membrane. Hollow Fiber Membrane MBRs consist of a bundle of fine hollow fibers, providing a large filtration surface area. Flat Sheet Membrane MBRs use a plane-like filtration material, allowing for simplified maintenance. Multi-Tubular Membrane MBRs contain multiple tubular membranes in a module, offering high resistance to fouling. These MBR types are widely used in wastewater treatment, enabling efficient separation of solids and microorganisms from the water.</p></p>
