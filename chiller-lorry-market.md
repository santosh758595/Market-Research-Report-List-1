<p><p>The chiller lorry market offers two main types: single temperature type and multi-temperature type. Single temperature type refers to trucks that are designed to transport goods at a constant temperature throughout the journey. These are typically used for transporting products that require a specific temperature range. On the other hand, multi-temperature type lorries are capable of accommodating different temperature zones within the same vehicle. This enables the transportation of various goods with different temperature requirements simultaneously, making them more versatile for diverse cargo needs.</p></p>
