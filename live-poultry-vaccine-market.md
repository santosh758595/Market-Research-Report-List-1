<p><p>The live poultry vaccine market consists of two main types: the injectable vaccine market and the spray vaccine market. Injectable vaccines are administered through injection, usually into the muscle or under the skin of the poultry. These vaccines provide immunity by allowing the birds' immune system to recognize and fight off the disease-causing pathogens. On the other hand, spray vaccines are delivered by spraying the vaccine directly onto the birds or into their environment. This method helps in easily reaching a large number of birds and provides protection by stimulating the immune response.</p></p>
