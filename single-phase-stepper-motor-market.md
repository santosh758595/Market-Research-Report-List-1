<p><p>The single-phase stepper motor market consists of three main types: Variable-reluctance (VR), Permanent Magnet (PM), and Hybrid (HB). VR motors utilize the principle of magnetic attraction, where the rotor aligns itself with the magnetic field of the stator. PM motors have a permanent magnet rotor and stator, creating a strong magnetic field. HB motors combine aspects of both VR and PM motors, with a permanent magnet rotor and a stator containing salient poles. These different types cater to the varying needs and applications of industries requiring precise and efficient motor control.</p></p>
