Cloud DDoS mitigation software identifies and filters out malicious traffic, allowing only legitimate traffic to reach the network.</p><p>The future of the cloud DDoS mitigation software market looks promising. With the increasing frequency and complexity of DDoS attacks, organizations are increasingly adopting cloud-based solutions to safeguard their networks. The shift towards cloud-based DDoS mitigation software is primarily driven by its scalability, flexibility, and cost-efficiency. Cloud-based solutions offer the ability to handle large traffic volumes and adapt to evolving attack techniques, ensuring uninterrupted operations.</p><p>Furthermore, the rise of IoT devices and the growing number of connected devices are also contributing to the demand for cloud DDoS mitigation software.
