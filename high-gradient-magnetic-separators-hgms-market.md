<p><p>High Gradient Magnetic Separators (HGMS) are magnetic separation systems used to extract and separate magnetic particles from a mixture. The market for HGMS is divided into two types: Wet High Gradient Magnetic Separators (WHGMS) and Dry High Gradient Magnetic Separators (DHGMS). WHGMS operate in a liquid medium and are often used in the mining industry. DHGMS, on the other hand, operate in a dry environment and are commonly used in industries such as recycling, ceramics, and food processing. Both types of separators effectively remove magnetic impurities from a mixture for various applications.</p></p>
