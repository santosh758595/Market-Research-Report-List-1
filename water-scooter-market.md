<p><p>A water scooter, also known as a personal watercraft, is a recreational watercraft that is designed for one or two persons. Firstly, there is an increasing demand for compact and lightweight water scooters that are easy to transport and store. Manufacturers are focusing on developing innovative designs and materials to meet this requirement. This trend aligns with the growing global concern for sustainable practices.</p><p>Furthermore, manufacturers are incorporating advanced safety features such as intelligent brake systems, keyless ignition, and anti-collision technologies to enhance rider safety and attract customers.
