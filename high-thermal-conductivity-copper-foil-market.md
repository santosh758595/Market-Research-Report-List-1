<p><p>The high thermal conductivity copper foil market comprises two main types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is produced through the rolling process, which involves passing copper through multiple sets of rollers to achieve the desired thickness and uniformity. This type of foil is commonly used in the electronics industry for manufacturing printed circuit boards. Electrolytic Copper Foil, on the other hand, is produced through an electrolysis process, resulting in a high-purity copper foil with excellent thermal conductivity and surface smoothness. It is widely used in applications such as lithium-ion batteries and electromagnetic shielding.</p></p>
