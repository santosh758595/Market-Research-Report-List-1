<p><p>Packaging machines for pouch market types include HFFS (Horizontal Form Fill Seal) machines, VFFS (Vertical Form Fill Seal) machines, and others. HFFS machines are used to form, fill, and seal horizontal pouches, making them suitable for products like cookies and candies. VFFS machines, on the other hand, form, fill, and seal vertical pouches, making them ideal for products like snacks and powders. The "Others" category encompasses packaging machines that do not fit into these two categories, such as stick pack machines used for single-serve products.</p></p>
