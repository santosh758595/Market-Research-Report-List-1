It exhibits excellent mechanical properties, high tensile strength, and resistance to abrasion, making it suitable for various applications in industries like automotive, electronics, and packaging. On the other hand, Nylon 12 is a thermoplastic polymer known for its low moisture absorption and excellent resistance to chemicals, making it ideal for applications such as fuel lines, cable sheathing, and coatings.</p></p>
