<p><p>The coated separator market consists of two main types: PVDF coated separator and ceramic coated separator. PVDF coated separators are made by applying a layer of polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF) on the separator to improve thermal stability and electrolyte wettability. These separators offer high performance and are widely used in lithium-ion batteries. On the other hand, ceramic coated separators are produced by coating the separator with a ceramic material to enhance mechanical strength, heat resistance, and safety features. These separators are gaining popularity in the battery industry due to their superior performance and safety advantages.</p></p>
