<p><p>The embossing powder market consists of two main types: transparent and opaque embossing powders. Transparent embossing powder is see-through, allowing the base color of the material to show through the powder when heated. It creates a subtle, glossy effect. On the other hand, opaque embossing powder covers the base color completely, providing a solid, vibrant finish. These powders are used in various crafts and art projects to add texture, dimension, and shine to stamped images or designs.</p></p>
