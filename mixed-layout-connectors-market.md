Pin contacts are male connectors with a protruding metal pin that fits into a corresponding socket contact. Socket contacts, on the other hand, are female connectors that receive the pin contacts. This allows for electrical connections between different components or devices. These two types of contacts are commonly used in various industries including aerospace, automotive, and telecommunications, where secure and reliable electrical connections are crucial.</p></p>
