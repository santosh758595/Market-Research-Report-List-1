<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Commercial Aircraft Battery Management System (BMS) market can be categorized into three types: centralized, modular, and distributed. In a centralized BMS, all battery management functions are integrated into a single control unit, providing a centralized management of the battery system. A modular BMS consists of multiple individual modules that control and monitor battery cells or groups of cells. Lastly, a distributed BMS has distributed electronics that are connected to each battery cell, enabling individual monitoring and control. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of aircraft manufacturers and operators.</p></p>
