<p><ul><li>Dual Function Pressure Switch</li><li>HPCO</li><li>Fan</li><li>Compressor Cycling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive AC pressure switch market consists of various types of switches used in air conditioning systems. The dual function pressure switch monitors both high and low pressures in the system and protects it from damage. The HPCO (High-Pressure Cutout) switch shuts off the compressor when the pressure exceeds a certain limit, preventing system failure. The fan switch controls the cooling fan's operation, ensuring efficient heat dissipation. The compressor cycling switch regulates the cycling of the compressor to maintain optimum cooling performance. These switches play vital roles in ensuring the smooth operation and performance of automotive AC systems.</p></p>
