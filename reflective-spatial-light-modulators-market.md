<p><p>Reflective Spatial Light Modulators (SLMs) are used to modulate or control light by reflecting it spatially. The Dielectric Mirror Type of SLM is built with layers of dielectric materials that reflect light of specific wavelengths, allowing fine control over the reflected light's phase and amplitude. On the other hand, the No-Dielectric Mirror Type SLM utilizes alternative reflective materials or structures, such as semiconductors, which may offer different performance characteristics. Both types cater to diverse applications requiring precise light modulation, but they differ in their underlying design and reflective properties.</p></p>
