<p><p>The dried cranberry market offers various types, including freeze-dried, air-dried, and others. Freeze-dried cranberries are created by freezing the fruit and then removing the water through a vacuum process, resulting in a light and crispy texture. Air-dried cranberries are obtained by dehydrating the fruit using warm air until the desired moisture level is achieved, resulting in a chewy texture. Other types may include cranberries dried using different techniques or mixed with additives for specific purposes. These different drying methods provide consumers with a range of texture and taste options when choosing dried cranberries.</p></p>
