<p><p>The Medical Metal 3D Printer market consists of various types, including FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling), FGF (Fused Granular Fabrication), LMF (Laser Metal Fusion), and others. FDM printers utilize a heated nozzle to extrude melted materials layer by layer. FGF printers employ a similar approach, but use granular feedstock instead. LMF printers fuse metal powder together using laser beams. The "others" category encompasses additional types that may utilize different techniques, such as binder jetting or electron beam melting. These technologies enable the production of medical-grade metal parts with high precision and complexity.</p></p>
