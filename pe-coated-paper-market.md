<p><p>PE coated paper, also known as polyethylene coated paper, is a type of paper that is coated with a layer of polyethylene on one or both sides. PE coated paper is often used in the production of cups, plates, food trays, and other disposable packaging products.</p><p>The future outlook of the PE coated paper market looks promising.
