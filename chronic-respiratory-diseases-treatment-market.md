<p><p>The chronic respiratory diseases treatment market includes different types of medications. Corticosteroids are anti-inflammatory drugs used to control inflammation in the airways. Bronchodilators help relax the muscles around the airways, making breathing easier. Monoclonal antibodies are biologic agents that target specific molecules involved in the inflammatory process. Antibiotics are used to treat respiratory infections. Mucolytic agents help break down and thin mucus in the airways. Leukotriene modifiers block the action of certain chemicals that cause inflammation. Other medications may include immunosuppressants, antitussives, or expectorants. These treatments aim to manage chronic respiratory conditions effectively.</p></p>
