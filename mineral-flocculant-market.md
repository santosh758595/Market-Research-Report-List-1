<p><p>The mineral flocculant market is categorized into various types such as activated silica, bentonite, metallic hydroxide, and others. Bentonite is another type of mineral flocculant that is commonly used in the oil and gas industry for drilling mud and waste management. Metallic hydroxides, on the other hand, are used for the treatment of industrial wastewater by precipitating heavy metals. Lastly, the "others" category includes mineral flocculants like alum, ferric chloride, and polyacrylamide.</p></p>
