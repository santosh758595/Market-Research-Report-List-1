<p><p>Knee bracing devices market comprises various types of braces that cater to different needs. Prophylactic braces are preventive in nature and are used to protect the knee from potential injuries during physical activities. Functional braces offer stability and support to individuals recovering from knee injuries or surgeries. Rehabilitative braces help in the rehabilitation process by limiting movement and providing support. Unloader braces are designed for individuals suffering from conditions like osteoarthritis and are meant to reduce pain and pressure on the knee joint during movement. These different types cater to specific requirements in the knee bracing devices market.</p></p>
