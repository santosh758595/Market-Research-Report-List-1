<p><strong>The Power Steering Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fit Filter</li><li>In-Line Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power steering filter market includes two types: direct fit filter and in-line filter. A direct fit filter is designed to fit and replace the original filter in power steering units without any modifications. It provides the same level of filtration and functionality as the OEM filter. On the other hand, an in-line filter is externally mounted and can be easily installed into the power steering system. It helps to prevent any contaminants from entering the system and ensures efficient performance of the power steering components.</p></p>
