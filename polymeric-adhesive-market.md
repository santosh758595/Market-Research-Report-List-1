<p><strong>The Polymeric Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The polymeric adhesive market can be classified into three main types. Thermosetting adhesives are heat-cured and form a cross-linked network, resulting in a strong and durable bond. On the other hand, thermoplastic adhesives can be reheated and re-melted multiple times, offering flexibility and reusability. Lastly, the "Others" category includes adhesives that do not fall strictly into the thermosetting or thermoplastic categories, such as pressure-sensitive adhesives that form an instant bond upon application. These different types cater to a wide range of applications and industries, providing various adhesive solutions to meet specific needs.</p></p>
