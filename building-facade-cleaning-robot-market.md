<p><p>The building facade cleaning robot market is divided into two types: contact cleaning robots and non-contact cleaning robots. Contact cleaning robots are designed to physically touch the building surface to remove dirt and debris through scrubbing or wiping. On the other hand, non-contact cleaning robots utilize advanced technologies such as high-pressure water jets or lasers to clean the facade without physical contact. Both types aim to automate the building cleaning process, providing efficient and cost-effective solutions while ensuring the cleanliness and maintenance of buildings.</p></p>
