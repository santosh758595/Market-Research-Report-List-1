<p><p>The Racquet Stringing Machine market is classified into two types: Manual and Electronic. Manual machines require physical effort to tension the strings and are typically less expensive. They are commonly used by amateur players or small-scale stringers. On the other hand, electronic machines use motors and sensors to precisely tension the strings. These machines are pricier but provide more accuracy and consistency, making them popular among professionals and high-volume stringers.
