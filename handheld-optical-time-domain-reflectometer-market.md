<p><p>The Handheld Optical Time Domain Reflectometer (OTDR) market can be divided into two types: Low Frequency Reflectometer and High Frequency Reflectometer. </p><p>Low Frequency Reflectometers operate at lower frequencies, providing a longer pulse width and a higher dynamic range. They are suitable for testing longer optical links, such as metropolitan and long-haul networks. </p><p>On the other hand, High Frequency Reflectometers operate at higher frequencies, offering a shorter pulse width and a lower dynamic range. They are ideal for testing shorter optical links found in local area networks and access networks.</p></p>
