<p><p>The vacuum truck market consists of three types: liquid suctioning only trucks, liquid and dry suctioning trucks, and high-velocity trucks. Liquid suctioning only trucks are designed to suction and transport liquids, such as wastewater or sludge. Liquid and dry suctioning trucks have the capability to suction both liquids and dry materials, such as sand or gravel. High-velocity trucks are specifically built to suction high volumes of solids or liquids at an accelerated rate. These trucks serve various industries, including construction, waste management, and oil and gas.</p></p>
