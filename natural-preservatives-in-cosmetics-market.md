<p><p>Natural preservatives in cosmetics market can be categorized into different types, including lipids, acids, alcohols, and others. Lipids, such as oils and waxes, are used to create a protective barrier that prevents microbial growth and spoilage. Acids, like citric acid and lactic acid, lower the pH level and create an unfavorable environment for bacteria. Alcohols, such as ethanol, act as antimicrobial agents by dehydrating microorganisms. Other natural preservatives may include plant extracts, essential oils, and antioxidants that inhibit the growth of bacteria and fungi while maintaining product stability.</p></p>
