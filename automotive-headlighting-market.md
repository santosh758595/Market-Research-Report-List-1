<p><p>The automotive headlighting market consists of three main types: halogen lighting, HID lighting, and LED lighting. Halogen lighting, the most common type, uses a filament to produce light. HID lighting, also known as xenon lighting, generates light through an electric arc. It offers brighter illumination and consumes less power. LED lighting, the most advanced type, uses light-emitting diodes to produce highly efficient and crisp illumination. LED lights are durable, energy-efficient, and have a longer lifespan than halogen or HID lights.</p></p>
