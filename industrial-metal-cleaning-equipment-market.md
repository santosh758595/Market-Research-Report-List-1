<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Industrial metal cleaning equipment can be categorized into two types: solvent metal cleaning equipment and aqueous metal cleaning equipment. Solvent metal cleaning equipment utilizes organic solvents to remove dirt, grease, and contaminants from metal surfaces. This method is effective for removing stubborn residues but may pose environmental and health risks. Aqueous metal cleaning equipment, on the other hand, employs water-based solutions along with detergents or additives to clean metal surfaces. This method is more environmentally friendly and safer for operators while still providing efficient cleaning results.</p></p>
