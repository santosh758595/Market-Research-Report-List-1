<p><p>Automotive Automatic Emergency Brake (AEB) is an advanced safety feature installed in vehicles that helps prevent collisions by automatically applying the brakes when a potential collision is detected. It uses sensors such as radar, cameras, and LIDAR to monitor the road ahead and detect objects or pedestrians in the vehicle's path. Once identified, the AEB system alerts the driver and if no action is taken, it applies the brakes to either reduce the severity of the collision or avoid it altogether.</p><p>The market analysis of the Automotive AEB market shows a significant growth potential.
