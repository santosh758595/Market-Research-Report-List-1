<p><p>The Blow-Fill-Seal (BFS) system is an advanced technology used in the pharmaceutical industry for the manufacturing of small-volume parenteral drugs. It is a one-step process that involves blow molding a container, filling it with the desired drug or solution, and sealing it in a sterile environment. This process ensures the integrity and safety of the product by minimizing human intervention and maintaining aseptic conditions.</p><p>The global Blow-Fill-Seal System market is projected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
