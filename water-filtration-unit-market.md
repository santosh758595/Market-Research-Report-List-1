<p><p>The water-filtration unit market offers various types of filters to cater to different needs. Bag and cartridge filters are effective for removing large particles from water. Activated carbon filters use charcoal to absorb impurities and improve taste. Media filters utilize sand or gravel to trap contaminants. Sediment filters remove solid particles like sand, silt, and rust. Reverse-osmosis filters employ a semi-permeable membrane to remove dissolved solids, chemicals, and microbes from water. These filters ensure clean and safe drinking water for households and industries.</p></p>
