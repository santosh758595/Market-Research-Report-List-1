<p><p>Brine Management Technology refers to various methods and processes used to effectively manage brine, a concentrated salt solution generated as a byproduct of desalination or resource extraction processes. The SAL-PROC process involves the removal of salts and impurities from water using electrochemical techniques. Zero discharge desalination focuses on minimizing or eliminating brine discharge, by either utilizing the brine for other purposes or treating it to remove harmful substances. Integrated processes combine multiple techniques, such as evaporation, crystallization, and solid-liquid separation, to maximize brine treatment efficiency and resource recovery.</p></p>
