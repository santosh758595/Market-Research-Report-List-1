<p><strong>The Outdoor BTS Antenna Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Directional Antenna</li><li>Omnidirectional Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Outdoor BTS Antenna Market refers to the market for antennas used in Base Transceiver Stations (BTS) located outdoors. Directional antennas are designed to focus the signal in a specific direction, providing long-range coverage and high gain. They are commonly used in rural areas or to cover specific areas with weak signal. On the other hand, omnidirectional antennas radiate the signal in all directions, providing 360-degree coverage with lower gain. They are typically utilized in urban or densely populated areas where signals are required in multiple directions.</p></p>
