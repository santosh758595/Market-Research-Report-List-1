<p><p>The aerospace and defense ducting market comprises three types: rigid, semi-rigid, and flexible ducting. Rigid ducting refers to fixed pipes or channels that maintain their shape and structure, offering a high level of durability and reliability. Semi-rigid ducting possesses moderate flexibility and can be bent to some extent but retains its general shape. On the other hand, flexible ducting is highly pliable, allowing it to be easily bent and routed through tight spaces. Each type of ducting has specific applications based on their unique characteristics and requirements in the aerospace and defense sectors.</p></p>
