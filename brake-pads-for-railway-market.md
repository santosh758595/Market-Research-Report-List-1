<p><p>Brake pads for railway market types include non-asbestos organic, ceramic, and semi-metallic options. Non-asbestos organic brake pads are made from a mixture of fibers, resins, and fillers, providing good stopping power, low noise, and less rotor wear. Ceramic brake pads, on the other hand, are composed of ceramic fibers embedded in a silicon compound, offering excellent heat resistance and durability with reduced noise. Lastly, semi-metallic brake pads contain metal fibers combined with other materials to enhance braking performance, durability, and heat dissipation. Different market types cater to varying needs and preferences.</p></p>
