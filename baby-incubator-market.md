<p><p>The baby incubator market consists of two main types: transport baby incubators and normal baby incubators. Transport baby incubators are designed for safe transportation of newborns from one location to another, equipped with advanced features like shock resistance and temperature control. On the other hand, normal baby incubators are stationary devices used in hospitals and neonatal care units to provide a controlled environment including temperature, humidity, and oxygen levels, ensuring optimal conditions for the baby's growth and development. These incubators contribute significantly to improving infant health and reducing mortality rates.</p></p>
