<p><strong>The Air Spring Assembly Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corrugated Bellows</li><li>Rolling Cam Bellows</li><li>Sleeve Bellows</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air spring assembly market consists of three main types: corrugated bellows, rolling cam bellows, and sleeve bellows. Corrugated bellows are flexible tubes made up of concentric pleats, allowing for expansion and contraction. Rolling cam bellows use a series of rolling cams to generate motion, providing a smoother ride. Sleeve bellows consist of an outer and inner sleeve, with air pressure creating the spring force. These different types of air springs offer unique advantages and applications in various industries, providing enhanced suspension, vibration isolation, and load-carrying capabilities.</p></p>
