<p><p>The amniotic product market can be categorized into three types: cryopreserved amniotic membranes, dehydrated amniotic membranes, and amniotic suspensions. Cryopreserved amniotic membranes are frozen and stored for later use in various medical procedures. Dehydrated amniotic membranes are processed to remove moisture content, creating a product that can be stored at room temperature. Amniotic suspensions involve the isolation and extraction of cells and growth factors from amniotic membranes to create a liquid formulation that can be injected into the targeted area for tissue regeneration and wound healing purposes.</p></p>
