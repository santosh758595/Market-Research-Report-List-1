<p><p>The screw conveyor market is categorized into three types based on installation: horizontal, inclined, and vertical. </p><p>Horizontal installation refers to the placement of the screw conveyor in a straight line parallel to the ground. It is commonly used for transporting bulk materials at a constant rate.</p><p>Inclined installation involves positioning the screw conveyor at an angle, allowing the materials to be moved upward or downward. This type is ideal for applications requiring elevation changes or inclines.</p><p>Vertical installation refers to the placement of the screw conveyor in a completely vertical position. It is suitable for lifting materials from a lower to a higher level, as well as for applications where space is limited.</p></p>
