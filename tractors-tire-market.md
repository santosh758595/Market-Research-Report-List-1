<p><p>The tractor tire market is classified into three types: Pneumatic, Solid, and Polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and provide an excellent balance of traction, cushioning, and grip. Solid tires, as the name suggests, are made of a solid rubber compound, offering durability, puncture resistance, and maintenance-free operation. Lastly, Polyurethane tires possess similar properties to solid tires but are typically lighter and provide more shock absorption. Each tire type caters to different agricultural needs, providing suitable options for farmers and tractor users.</p></p>
