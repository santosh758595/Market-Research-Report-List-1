<p><p>The Dye Sublimation Inks Market offers two main types of inks: water-based and oil-based. Water-based dye sublimation ink is composed of water as a solvent, making it environmentally friendly and user-friendly. It is typically used for printing on various materials, including textiles, ceramics, and paper. On the other hand, oil-based sublimation ink utilizes oil as a solvent, providing better color saturation and durability. It is commonly employed for printing on fabrics and other porous materials. Consequently, these market segments cater to different preferences and requirements in the dye sublimation industry.</p></p>
