<p><p>The fertilizer nutrient market encompasses various types of fertilizers that provide essential nutrients for plant growth. Nitrogen-based fertilizers primarily contain nitrogen, which promotes leaf and stem development. Phosphorus-based fertilizers contain phosphorus, crucial for root development and flowering. Potassium-based fertilizers contain potassium, supporting overall plant health and disease resistance. Calcium fertilizers provide calcium, benefiting cell wall strength and fruit development. Micronutrient fertilizers contain trace elements like iron, zinc, and manganese, necessary for enzyme activity and healthy plant functions. These different types cater to specific nutrient requirements of plants, aiding proper growth and productivity.</p></p>
