<p><p>The Fiberglass Yarn Market consists of two main types: Fiber Yarn and Fiber Roving. Fiber Yarn refers to the continuous strands of glass fibers that are twisted or plied together to form a single thread-like structure. It is used in various applications like textile reinforcement, insulation, and filtration. On the other hand, Fiber Roving consists of untwisted bundles of continuous glass filaments. It is commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction for its high tensile strength and heat resistance properties.</p></p>
