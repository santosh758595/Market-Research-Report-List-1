<p><p>The dewatering pumps market can be classified into two main types: submersible dewatering pumps and non-submersible dewatering pumps. Submersible dewatering pumps are designed to be fully submerged in water or other liquids and are commonly used in applications such as sewage systems or underground mines. On the other hand, non-submersible dewatering pumps are used in situations where the pump needs to be placed above water level, such as construction sites or drainage systems. These pumps are versatile and efficient in removing excess water from various locations.</p></p>
