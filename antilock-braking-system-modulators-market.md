<p><p>Antilock Braking System (ABS) modulators are categorized into four types: one-channel ABS, two-channel ABS, three-channel ABS, and four-channel ABS. </p><p>A one-channel ABS modulator is designed to control the braking function of a single wheel. The two-channel ABS modulator manages the braking system of two wheels, usually one on each axle. Similarly, a three-channel ABS modulator controls the braking of three wheels, typically one on the rear axle and two on the front axle. The four-channel ABS modulator controls the braking of all four wheels individually, providing the highest level of stability and control during braking.</p></p>
