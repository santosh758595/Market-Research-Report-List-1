<p><p>The automotive lamp pumps market can be categorized into two segments - OEM and aftermarket. OEM stands for Original Equipment Manufacturer. In this segment, the lamp pumps are supplied directly by the vehicle manufacturer to be installed during the production of the vehicle. On the other hand, the aftermarket segment refers to the lamp pumps that are purchased and installed by the vehicle owner or authorized service centers after the vehicle has been produced. Both segments cater to the demand for automotive lamp pumps, but they differ in terms of the distribution channel and timing of installation.</p></p>
