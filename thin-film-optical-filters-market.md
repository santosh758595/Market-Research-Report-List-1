<p><p>The Thin-Film Optical Filters market consists of various types of filters. Bandpass Filters allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass through while blocking others. Notch Filters work in the opposite way, blocking a specific range while allowing others to pass. Shortpass Edge Filters transmit wavelengths below a particular cutoff point while Longpass Edge Filters transmit wavelengths above a certain threshold. Dichroic Filters are designed to reflect specific wavelengths while transmitting others. These filters find applications in various industries such as telecommunications, consumer electronics, and healthcare.</p></p>
