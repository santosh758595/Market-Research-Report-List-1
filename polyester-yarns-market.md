<p><p>Polyester yarns are synthetic fibers made by combining ethylene glycol and terephthalic acid. They are widely used in the textile industry to produce various types of fabrics, including apparel, home furnishings, and industrial textiles. Polyester yarns offer superior strength, durability, and resistance to stretching, shrinking, and wrinkling compared to natural fibers like cotton or wool. They also have excellent moisture-wicking properties and can be blended with other fibers to enhance performance characteristics.</p><p>The polyester yarns market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The market analysis indicates that the increasing demand for polyester yarns in the textile industry is primarily driving this growth. The rising population and changing fashion trends have led to a surge in demand for apparel, home furnishings, and other textile products, thereby boosting the demand for polyester yarns.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing a shift towards eco-friendly and sustainable polyester yarns. Manufacturers are adopting recycled polyester and bio-based polyester yarns to meet the growing demand for environmentally friendly products. This trend is expected to further propel the market growth.</p><p>In addition, the polyester yarns market is witnessing technological advancements and innovations to improve the performance characteristics of the yarns. Manufacturers are investing in R&D activities to develop enhanced polyester yarns with properties like antibacterial, flame-retardant, and ultra-violet protection. These advancements are expected to create new growth opportunities in the market.</p><p>Overall, the polyester yarns market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for polyester yarns in the textile industry, the shift towards sustainable polyester yarns, and technological advancements are the key factors driving this growth.</p></p>
