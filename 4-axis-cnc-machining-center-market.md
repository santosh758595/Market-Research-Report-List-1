<p><p>A 4-axis CNC machining center refers to a computer-controlled machine used in various industries for precision cutting, shaping, and milling of materials. There are two main types: Vertical Machining Centers (VMCs) and Horizontal Machining Centers (HMCs). VMCs have a vertically oriented spindle that moves along the Z-axis, making it suitable for tasks like drilling, tapping, and pocketing. On the other hand, HMCs have a horizontally oriented spindle that moves along the X and Y axes, making it ideal for larger workpieces and multi-sided machining tasks.</p></p>
