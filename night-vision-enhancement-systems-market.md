<p><p>The night vision enhancement systems market can be categorized into two types: Near-infrared (NIR) and Far-infrared (FIR). Near-infrared technology uses light that falls just outside the visible spectrum, enabling better visibility in low-light conditions. It is commonly used in consumer electronics like surveillance cameras. On the other hand, Far-infrared technology detects heat emitted by objects, allowing for clear vision in complete darkness. This technology is often used in military applications, such as thermal imaging goggles and scopes. Both NIR and FIR systems contribute to improving vision in low-light environments.</p></p>
