<p><p>The Aircraft Flight Management Systems (FMS) market includes different types of Control Display Units (CDU) and Multifunction Control Display Units (MCDU). A CDU is a device used by pilots to input flight data into the FMS and displays information related to aircraft navigation, flight planning, and performance calculations. An MCDU is an advanced version of CDU, offering additional functionalities like graphical interfaces and touchscreen capabilities. Both CDU and MCDU play a crucial role in managing and optimizing aircraft flight operations, assisting pilots in navigating and controlling the aircraft efficiently.</p></p>
