<p><p>The recreational vehicle (RV) awning market consists of two types: manual and electric awnings. These awnings are more affordable and suitable for those who prefer a more hands-on approach. On the other hand, electric awnings are operated with the help of a motorized system, allowing for easy extension and retraction with the push of a button. Electric awnings are more convenient but tend to be more expensive than manual ones.</p></p>
