<p><p>Connected smart thermostats come in two different market types: wired and wireless. Wired thermostats are connected directly to the home's electrical system and typically require professional installation. They are widely regarded as more reliable and stable due to their wired connection. On the other hand, wireless thermostats are connected to the home's Wi-Fi network and offer more flexibility in terms of placement and control. While wireless thermostats may be easier to install and reposition, they may also be slightly less reliable due to potential signal interference or connection issues.</p></p>
