Each type serves a specific purpose in the semiconductor manufacturing process. The Semiconductor Gas Filter removes impurities from gases used in the manufacturing process. The Semiconductor Water Filter eliminates impurities from water, which is used for cooling and cleaning purposes. The Semiconductor Chemical Filter is responsible for purifying chemicals used in the manufacturing process. Lastly, the Semiconductor CMP Filter removes particles during the chemical mechanical planarization (CMP) process.</p></p>
