<p><p>The Insulin Delivery System market is comprised of three main types: Insulin Syringes, Insulin Pumps, and Insulin Pens. Insulin Syringes are traditional devices that allow the user to manually inject insulin into their body. Insulin Pumps are small devices that deliver a continuous supply of insulin through a catheter inserted under the skin. Insulin Pens are handheld devices that combine a pen-like design with replaceable insulin cartridges, offering a convenient and discreet method of insulin delivery. These different types provide options for individuals with different preferences and needs for managing their diabetes.</p></p>
