<p><p>Blood glucose monitoring is an essential aspect of managing diabetes. The market for monitoring devices can be categorized into wearable and implantable types. On the other hand, implantable glucose monitoring devices involve inserting a sensor beneath the skin to continuously measure glucose levels.
