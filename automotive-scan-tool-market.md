<p><p>The automotive scan tool market can be categorized into two types: OBD 2 scan tool and OBD 3 scan tool. The OBD 2 scan tool is designed to diagnose and monitor the performance of vehicles using the On-Board Diagnostics 2 system. It provides real-time data on engine parameters and identifies any faults or issues. On the other hand, the OBD 3 scan tool is an advanced version that offers enhanced diagnostic capabilities, including access to more in-depth vehicle information and increased compatibility with newer vehicles.</p></p>
