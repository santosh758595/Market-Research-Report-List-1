<p><p>A Planar Inverted F Antenna (PIFA) is a type of compact antenna that is widely used in wireless communication devices. It consists of a metal plate, typically in the shape of an inverted "F," which is embedded in a planar ground plane. PIFA antennas are known for their small size, low profile, and omnidirectional radiation pattern, making them suitable for integration in portable devices such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops.</p><p>The PIFA market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for wireless communication devices and the growing adoption of Internet of Things (IoT) technology.
