<p><p>The PET Nuclear Medicine Imaging market comprises various types of imaging techniques, including PET-CT, PET-MRI, and others. PET-CT combines Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Computed Tomography (CT) to provide detailed anatomical and functional information simultaneously. PET-MRI, on the other hand, combines PET with Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) to offer high-resolution anatomical and functional imaging. Other types of imaging techniques may include standalone PET systems or hybrid combinations with different imaging modalities. These different market segments cater to the specific diagnostic needs and preferences of healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
