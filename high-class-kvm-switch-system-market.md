<p><p>The High Class KVM Switch System market can be categorized into two types: Analog High Class KVM Switch System and Digital High Class KVM Switch System. </p><p>Analog High Class KVM Switch Systems utilize analog signals to transfer video, keyboard, and mouse data among multiple computers. These systems are ideal for legacy systems that do not provide digital output.</p><p>On the other hand, Digital High Class KVM Switch Systems utilize digital signals for transmitting data, delivering higher video quality and faster response times. They are suitable for modern systems that offer digital output options.</p><p>Both types of KVM Switch Systems provide efficient control and management of multiple computers from a single console.</p></p>
