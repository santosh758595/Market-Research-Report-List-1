<p><p>A Bidi (Bidirectional) Transceiver is a type of optical transceiver that is capable of transmitting and receiving data over a single optical fiber. Bidi transceivers are known for their ability to maximize the utilization of optical fibers and minimize the complexity and cost of cabling infrastructure.</p><p>The Bidi Transceiver Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for high-speed communication networks and the need for efficient utilization of optical fibers are the major factors driving the market growth.
