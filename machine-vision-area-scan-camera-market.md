<p><p>The machine vision area scan camera market comprises two main types: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras are used to capture images line by line, making them suitable for applications where continuous imaging of moving objects is required, like inspection of high-speed production lines. Area scan cameras, on the other hand, capture a complete image of a given area at once, making them ideal for applications that require detailed analysis of still objects, such as quality control in manufacturing or biometric identification. Both types cater to different needs within the machine vision industry.</p></p>
