<p><p>LED Diffuse Reflective Photoelectric Sensors are used for object detection in various industries. The market types of these sensors can be divided into Digital Signal and Analog Signal. Digital Signal sensors provide a binary output, usually in the form of an on/off signal, indicating the presence or absence of an object. Analog Signal sensors, on the other hand, provide a continuous output that varies proportionally with the detected object's distance or reflectivity. Both types offer different advantages and can be preferred based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
