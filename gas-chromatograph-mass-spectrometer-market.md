<p><p>The Gas Chromatograph-Mass Spectrometer (GC-MS) market can be broadly classified into two types: Stationary GC-MS and Portable GC-MS. Stationary GC-MS systems are large and typically used in laboratories for extensive analysis, offering high accuracy and sensitivity. They allow for complex sample separation and identification processes. On the other hand, Portable GC-MS systems are compact and can be easily transported to remote locations. They are suitable for on-field analysis, providing quick results and enabling real-time monitoring in various industries such as environmental, pharmaceutical, and food safety.</p></p>
