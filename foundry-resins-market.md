<p><strong>The Foundry Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Medium Nitrogen Resin</li><li>Medium and Low Nitrogen Resin</li><li>Low Nitrogen Resin</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foundry resins are categorized into different types based on their nitrogen content. Medium nitrogen resin, as the name suggests, contains a moderate level of nitrogen and is used in foundry sand molds to improve dimensional accuracy and surface finish. Medium and low nitrogen resin combines both medium and low nitrogen content, offering a balance of properties suitable for various casting applications. Low nitrogen resin contains a minimal nitrogen content and is ideal for foundry cores and molds requiring high strength and low gas evolution. Other types of foundry resins refer to those not falling under the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
