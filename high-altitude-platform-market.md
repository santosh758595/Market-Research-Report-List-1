UAVs, commonly known as drones, are aircraft operated remotely without a pilot onboard. Airships are large, self-powered aircraft that use gas-filled compartments to remain airborne. Tethered aerostat systems are stationary balloons or blimps that are connected to the ground by a cable and can carry various equipment and payloads. These platforms offer a range of applications such as communication, surveillance, and weather monitoring at high altitudes.</p></p>
