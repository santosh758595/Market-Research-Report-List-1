<p><p>There are three main types of PETG markets: extruded grade, injection molding grade, and blow molding grade. Extruded grade PETG is used for producing sheets and films with excellent clarity and toughness. Injection molding grade PETG is suitable for creating complex shaped objects, such as bottles and containers, with good mechanical properties. Blow molding grade PETG is specifically designed for blow molding applications, enabling the production of large containers and hollow products with high clarity and impact resistance. These different grades cater to specific manufacturing processes and end-use applications.</p></p>
