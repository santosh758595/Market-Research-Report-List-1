<p><p>The portable mini Bluetooth speakers market can be divided into three types: AC/DC Bluetooth speakers, AC-only Bluetooth speakers, and DC-only Bluetooth speakers. AC/DC Bluetooth speakers can be powered by both alternating current (AC) and direct current (DC), providing flexibility for use in different settings. AC-only Bluetooth speakers require an AC power source for operation, making them suitable for indoor use. On the other hand, DC-only Bluetooth speakers rely solely on a DC power source, often provided by batteries, making them ideal for outdoor activities where an AC power supply may not be available.</p></p>
