<p><ul><li>Valved Breather</li><li>Unvalved Breather</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PM2.5 breather market can be divided into two types: valved breathers and unvalved breathers. Valved breathers are equipped with valves that allow for easy inhaling and exhaling, as the valves open when inhaling and close when exhaling. This design ensures efficient air circulation and reduces breathing resistance. On the other hand, unvalved breathers do not have valves, which means the air has to be forced through the filter, resulting in higher breathing resistance. The choice between these two types depends on personal preference and the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
