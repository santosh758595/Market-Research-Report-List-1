<p><p>Lubricated vacuum pumps can be classified into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage. Single-stage pumps have a single pump mechanism that creates vacuum by compressing gases. These pumps are typically used for applications that require low to moderate levels of vacuum. On the other hand, multi-stage pumps have multiple pump mechanisms that allow for higher levels of vacuum. They are designed for applications that demand a higher degree of vacuum, such as in scientific research, pharmaceutical manufacturing, and the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
