<p><p>Non-silicone mold release agents are available in two main forms: non-silicone liquid release agents and non-silicone aerosol release agents. The non-silicone liquid release agents are formulated in liquid form and are applied manually onto the mold surface. They provide a thin film that acts as a barrier between the mold and the substrate, allowing easy release of the molded object. On the other hand, non-silicone aerosol release agents are packaged in aerosol cans and are applied through spraying. They offer convenience and ease of application compared to the liquid form.</p></p>
