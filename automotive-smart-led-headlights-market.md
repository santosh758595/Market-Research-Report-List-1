<p><p>The automotive smart LED headlights market can be classified into two main types: the adaptive headlight system and the adaptive high beam system. Adaptive headlight systems adjust the lighting direction based on the vehicle's speed and steering angle, providing better illumination in curves and turns. On the other hand, adaptive high beam systems automatically switch between high and low beams based on surrounding traffic conditions, ensuring optimal visibility without dazzling other drivers. These technologies enhance safety and improve visibility for drivers, making nighttime driving safer and more efficient.</p></p>
