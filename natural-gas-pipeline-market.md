<p><p>The natural gas pipeline market consists of three main types: gathering pipelines, transportation pipelines, and distribution pipelines. Gathering pipelines are used to collect natural gas from wells and transport it to processing plants or transmission pipelines. Transportation pipelines then carry the gas long distances, often spanning several states or even countries. Lastly, distribution pipelines deliver the natural gas to end-users such as homes, businesses, and industrial facilities. Each market type plays a crucial role in the overall natural gas supply chain, ensuring the efficient and reliable delivery of this important energy source.</p></p>
