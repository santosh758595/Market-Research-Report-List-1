<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Medical Gastrointestinal Stent market?</strong></p>
<p><p>Some emerging trends in the global medical gastrointestinal stent market include the increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures, the development of bioresorbable stents, and the growing demand for self-expandable stents.
