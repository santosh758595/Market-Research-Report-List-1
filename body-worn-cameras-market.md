<p><p>Body-Worn Cameras (BWCs) are small devices worn by law enforcement officers, security personnel, and other professionals, usually on their uniforms. Moreover, the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms in BWCs helps in automating video analysis, which reduces the time and effort required for manual review.</p><p>Another trend seen in the market is the adoption of cloud-based storage solutions.
