<p><p>The power-shift tractor market can be categorized into two types: Fullpower Shift and Semi-Power Shift. In a Fullpower Shift market, the tractor is equipped with a transmission system that allows for smooth and seamless shifting from one gear to another, without the need to stop or clutch. This enhances efficiency and productivity in heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, the Semi-Power Shift market offers a transmission system that requires partial shifting interruption during gear changes, making it suitable for less demanding tasks. Both market types cater to different operational needs and requirements.</p></p>
