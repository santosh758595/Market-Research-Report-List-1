<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vanadium targets are predominantly used in the field of physical vapor deposition (PVD) techniques, specifically in the manufacturing of thin films on various surfaces. Plane targets refer to the stationary flat-shaped vanadium target materials that are ideal for large-scale production and can cover a wider surface area during deposition. On the other hand, rotating targets involve cylindrical vanadium targets that are spun during the deposition process, allowing for more precise and controlled film deposition on a smaller surface area. Both types cater to different needs in the PVD industry.</p></p>
