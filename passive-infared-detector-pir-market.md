<p><p>The Passive Infrared Detector (PIR) market comprises two main types: singal-beam and multi-beam detectors. Singal-beam detectors use a single infrared beam to detect motion. They are popular in small-scale applications like home security systems. On the other hand, multi-beam detectors use multiple infrared beams and offer enhanced accuracy and coverage. They are commonly used in larger-scale applications, such as commercial buildings and outdoor spaces, where a wider detection range is required. Both types play a crucial role in the market, catering to different needs and requirements of various customers.</p></p>
