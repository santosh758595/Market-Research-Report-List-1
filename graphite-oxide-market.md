<p><p>Graphite oxide refers to a compound that is formed by the oxidation of graphite. Graphite oxide is known for its unique properties, including good water dispersibility, electrical conductivity, and mechanical flexibility.</p><p>The graphite oxide market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the growing demand for graphene, a derivative of graphite oxide.
