<p><p>The market for large diameter stainless steel pipes can be classified into two main types: welded stainless steel pipes and seamless stainless steel pipes. Welded stainless steel pipes are manufactured by welding two or more stainless steel plates together, offering enhanced strength and durability. On the other hand, seamless stainless steel pipes are produced through a seamless process, resulting in a smoother interior surface and better resistance to pressure. Both types of pipes are widely used in various industries such as oil and gas, construction, and automotive.</p></p>
