<p><p>The edible preservatives market is divided into various types. Organic waxes are also used as preservatives, such as shellac and beeswax, which create a protective coating on food surfaces. Lastly, there are other types of preservatives like antioxidants and antimicrobials that offer different mechanisms to extend the shelf life of food products.</p></p>
