Fluorescence Plate Readers utilize fluorescence emission to measure the concentration of target molecules in samples. Absorbance Plate Readers measure the absorption of light by samples to determine their concentration. Luminescence Plate Readers measure the emission of light from samples, usually caused by chemical reactions, to analyze their concentration. These different types provide researchers with various options for measuring and analyzing samples in laboratory settings.</p></p>
