<p><p>The hydraulic braking system market is categorized into three types: hydraulic disc brakes, hydraulic drum brakes, and others. Hydraulic disc brakes use calipers to squeeze brake pads against a rotor to slow down or stop the vehicle, providing excellent stopping power and heat dissipation. Hydraulic drum brakes use hydraulic force to expand brake shoes against the drum, offering reliable stopping performance and durability. The "others" category includes emerging technologies and variations of hydraulic braking systems, such as regenerative braking or electronically controlled hydraulic brakes.</p></p>
