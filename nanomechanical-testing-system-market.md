<p><p>The Nanomechanical Testing System market comprises various types of equipment used for nanoscale testing and analysis. Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs) utilize electron beams to image and analyze materials at the atomic level. Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs) provide high-resolution images and surface analysis using focused electron beams. Dual-Beam (FIB/SEM) Systems integrate both focused ion beams and electron beams for advanced material characterization. Spectroscopes enable the measurement of the interaction between materials and electromagnetic radiation, providing valuable information about molecular structures and chemical compositions.</p></p>
