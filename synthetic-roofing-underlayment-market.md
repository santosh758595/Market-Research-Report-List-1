<p><p>Synthetic roofing underlayment is a material used to protect the roof during installation and acts as a secondary barrier against moisture. It can be classified into different types based on their composition. Asphalt-saturated felt is a traditional type made from paper saturated with asphalt. Rubberized asphalt underlayment has a layer of rubberized asphalt for enhanced durability and protection. Non-bitumen synthetic underlayment is composed of materials like polypropylene or polyester and offers superior resistance to tears and UV damage. Other types may include specialized underlayments with additional properties such as fire resistance or sound insulation.</p></p>
