<p><ul><li>Bituminous Tape</li><li>Butyl Rubber Tape</li><li>Liquid Silicone Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roof repair tape market consists of three main types of tapes: bituminous tape, butyl rubber tape, and liquid silicone tape. Bituminous tape is made from a mixture of asphalt and adhesive materials, providing a durable and waterproof solution. Butyl rubber tape offers excellent adhesion and flexibility, making it ideal for sealing joints and leaks. Liquid silicone tape, on the other hand, forms a protective coating when applied and cures into a solid rubber, offering long-lasting and weather-resistant repairs. Each type caters to different repair needs and provides effective solutions for repairing roofs.</p></p>
