<p><p>Coextruded multi-layer film is a type of packaging material which is made by extruding multiple layers of polymers simultaneously. It provides excellent barrier properties, high strength, and flexibility. The layers of the film are typically made of different materials, each offering specific properties like oxygen and moisture barriers, heat resistance, and clarity. This makes coextruded multi-layer film a preferred choice for various applications in industries such as food and beverages, personal care, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture.</p><p>The coextruded multi-layer film market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for flexible packaging solutions.
