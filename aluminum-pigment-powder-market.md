<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The aluminum pigment powder market can be divided into two types: leafing aluminum pigment powder and non-leafing aluminum pigment powder. Leafing aluminum pigment powder consists of flakes that stay on the surface of the material, creating a metallic appearance. This type is commonly used in applications where a high luster and reflectivity are desired. On the other hand, non-leafing aluminum pigment powder does not float on the surface but gets dispersed evenly throughout the material, resulting in a more subtle metallic effect. This type is typically employed when a smooth and matte finish is desired.</p></p>
