Cultured marble is a blend of crushed limestone and resin that can be molded into various shapes like bathtubs and vanity tops. Engineered stone, also known as quartz, is made by combining crushed quartz with resin and offers a beautiful and durable alternative to natural stone for countertops and other architectural surfaces.</p></p>
