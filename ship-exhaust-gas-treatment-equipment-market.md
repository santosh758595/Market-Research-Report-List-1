<p><p>Ship Exhaust Gas Treatment Equipment, also known as scrubber systems, is used on ships to reduce air pollution caused by the emission of harmful gases from the ship's engines. This helps ships comply with international emission standards and regulations.</p><p>The ship exhaust gas treatment equipment market has been experiencing significant growth due to the increasing concern for environmental pollution caused by ship emissions, as well as stringent emission regulations imposed by governments and international organizations. One of the trends is the adoption of hybrid scrubber systems, which combine both open-loop and closed-loop technologies to provide flexibility in complying with different emission regulations in various regions.
