<p><p>The functional near-infrared optical brain imaging system (fNIRS) market consists of various types including FT-NIR spectroscopy, dispersive NIR spectroscopy, and others such as acousto-optic tunable filters (AOTF) and filters. FT-NIR spectroscopy utilizes Fourier transform technology to analyze the brain function. Dispersive NIR spectroscopy uses grating and prism to disperse light for brain imaging. AOTF and filter-based systems use advanced optical mechanisms to extract relevant brain data. These different types offer unique features and capabilities for studying brain activity and are utilized in various research and clinical applications.</p></p>
