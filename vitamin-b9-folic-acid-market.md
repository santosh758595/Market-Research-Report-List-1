<p><p>Vitamin B9, also known as folic acid or folate, is a water-soluble vitamin that plays a crucial role in various bodily functions. The demand for folic acid is expected to grow steadily due to the increasing awareness about its health benefits and the rising prevalence of vitamin B9 deficiencies. Folic acid supplements are commonly prescribed to pregnant women to prevent neural tube defects in their babies. Consequently, the growing emphasis on prenatal healthcare and the rising global birth rate are expected to drive the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, folic acid is known to have potential benefits in cardiovascular health, mental health, and the prevention of certain cancers. This has led to a surge in consumer interest and adoption of folic acid supplements, further contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the food and beverage industry is increasingly incorporating folic acid into products to meet the growing consumer demand for functional foods.
