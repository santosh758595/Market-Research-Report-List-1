<p><p>The high pressure sealant market consists of two main types: hydraulic high pressure sealants and pneumatic high pressure sealants. On the other hand, pneumatic high pressure sealants are designed for use in pneumatic systems that also operate under high pressure. They serve a similar purpose of preventing leakage and maintaining system efficiency. Both types of sealants are crucial for industries that require high pressure operations.</p></p>
