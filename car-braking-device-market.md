<p><p>The car braking device market consists of two main types: the Two-Box System and the One-Box System. The Two-Box System is composed of two separate components, the master cylinder and the brake booster, which work together to provide efficient and reliable braking performance. On the other hand, the One-Box System integrates both the master cylinder and the brake booster into a single unit, offering a more compact and space-saving design. Both systems aim to enhance vehicle safety by ensuring optimal braking control and responsiveness.</p></p>
