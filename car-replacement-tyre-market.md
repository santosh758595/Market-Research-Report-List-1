<p><p>The car replacement tyre market consists of two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires are constructed with layers of cord running at right angles to the direction of travel, resulting in better grip, durability, and fuel efficiency. These tires have become more popular due to their technological advancements. On the other hand, bias tires have cords running diagonally across the direction of travel, providing a smoother ride but less traction and fuel efficiency. While bias tires are still used in certain applications like off-road or vintage cars, radial tires dominate the modern car replacement tyre market.</p></p>
