There are two types of pH controllers: Acid Donor market and Alkali Donor market. Acid Donor market controllers release acid into the system to decrease the pH levels, while Alkali Donor market controllers release alkali to increase the pH levels. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the optimal pH conditions required for different textile processes, such as dyeing, bleaching, and printing, ensuring high-quality and consistent results.</p></p>
