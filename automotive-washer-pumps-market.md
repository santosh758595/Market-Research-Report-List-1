<p><p>Automotive Washer Pumps market includes two types - mono pump and dual pump. A mono pump system consists of a single pump that is responsible for both front and rear windshield cleaning functions in a vehicle. On the other hand, a dual pump system includes two pumps, one for the front windshield and another for the rear windshield. The dual pump system provides independent control over the cleaning functions of the front and rear windshields, allowing for more flexibility and efficient cleaning operations.</p></p>
