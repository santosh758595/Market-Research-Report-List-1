<p><p>The mineral wool material market offers various types of products. Felty mineral wool is a dense and highly fibrous material used for insulation and soundproofing. Strip mineral wool comes in long, thin strips and is commonly used for sealing gaps and joints in buildings. Tubular mineral wool is similar but in a tube shape, commonly used as pipe insulation. Granular mineral wool consists of small particles and is used for loose-fill insulation. Plate mineral wool is a rigid and flat material commonly used for thermal and acoustic insulation in walls and ceilings.</p></p>
